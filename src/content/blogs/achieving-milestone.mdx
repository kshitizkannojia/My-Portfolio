# Finally, a Blog Section!

It happened. Between building SaaS apps, writing cold-email AI pipelines, and scanning invoices with OCR — I somehow found time to add a blog to my portfolio. Priorities? Never heard of them.

## Why It Took So Long

Step 1: "I should start a blog." Step 2: Spend three months building projects instead of writing about them. Step 3: Realize the portfolio has a blog feature built in. Step 4: Actually write something.

Here we are at Step 4.

## What You'll Find Here

I'm a CSE student at M.S. Engineering College, Bengaluru, currently in my final year. I've interned at MindMatrix (Android + GenAI) and Japfa (Flutter), and I build full-stack apps in my free time — React, FastAPI, Supabase, Flutter, you name it.

So expect posts about:

- **Things I built** and why certain technical decisions made sense (or didn't).
- **Lessons from internships** — what textbooks skip and production code teaches.
- **The tech behind this portfolio** — 3D keyboards, GSAP animations, and way too many npm packages.

## The Space Theme Though

Dark background, floating particles, a 3D keyboard that lights up when you hover on it. Is it overkill for a portfolio? Absolutely. Do I love it? Also absolutely.
